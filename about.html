The Problem We Solve

FINDING A FIGHT CAMP
USED TO BE BROKEN

Before CheckFightCamp, fighters faced the same frustrating experience every time they tried to find a camp.

💸
Hidden Booking Fees

Other platforms charge camps to be listed — and charge YOU a booking fee on top. The best camps often weren't listed at all.

❓
No Transparent Pricing

Camp websites rarely showed clear pricing for different durations, private training, or accommodation. You had to email and wait.

♀
No Women-Specific Filters

Female fighters had no reliable way to find camps with female trainers, women-only classes, or safe accommodation options.

How We Help

WHY CHECKFIGHTCAMP IS DIFFERENT

01
100% Free, Always

We never charge fighters a booking fee. We never charge camps to appear in search results. Our listings are based purely on quality and completeness of information. If a camp is great, it shows up — period.

02
Transparent Pricing by Duration

See exactly what you'll pay for 1 day, 1 week, 1 month or 3 months — for group classes AND private training. In your currency. No surprises, no emails required.

03
Women & Safety First

Every camp is specifically assessed for female trainers, women-only classes, safe neighbourhood and 24h security. Female fighters get their own dedicated filter so they can find camps where they'll thrive.

04
Verified, Not Paid

Our team manually verifies every camp listing. We check websites, review real fighter feedback, and update information regularly. If something's wrong, we fix it — not because a camp paid us, but because accuracy matters.

05
Compare Side by Side

Select up to 3 camps and compare them across every dimension — price, location, disciplines, accommodation, beginner-friendliness and more. Make a confident decision without endless tab-switching.
