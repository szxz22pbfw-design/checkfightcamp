The Problem We Solve

WAS BROKEN

Before CheckFightCamp, fighters faced the same frustrating experience every time they tried to find a camp.

Hidden Commissions

Other platforms charge camps to be listed — meaning the best camps often weren't listed at all. Ranking was pay-to-play.

❓
No Transparent Pricing

Camp websites rarely showed clear pricing for different durations, private training, or accommodation. You had to email and wait days.

🔎

How We Help

WHY CHECKFIGHTCAMP
IS DIFFERENT

01
No Commissions, Ever

We never take commissions from camps or fighters. Every listing is based purely on quality and completeness of information — not who paid us more.

02
Transparent Pricing by Duration

See exactly what you'll pay for 1 day, 1 week, 1 month or 3 months — for group classes and private training. No emails required.

03
Verified, Not Paid

Our team manually verifies every camp listing. We check websites, review real fighter feedback and update information regularly. Accuracy matters.

04
Compare Side by Side

05
Map + Filter in One Place
